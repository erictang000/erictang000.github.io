Hi! I'm Eric Tang, a rising senior at UC Berkeley studying EECS. I'm interested in all things CS, but most recently I've enjoyed exploring machine learning safety, and the applications of ML in physical sciences. I also enjoy teaching data structures and algorithms, and have TA'd 5 times for CS 61B. In my free time outside of computer science activities I also like to run, read, and play/follow basketball.

This site will host some of the teaching resources for 61B I've compiled over the last 4 semesters, some course and personal projects that I thought were cool, and hopefully some blog posts about what I've been reading/thinking about.
